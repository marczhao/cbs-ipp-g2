About Us

Happy Consultant GIFs was founded by Marc Zhao and Davy Zhang in Oct 2016, both of whom recently recieved offers from BCG Greater China Area. As consultants to be, Marc and Davy have been devoting their lifes to find solutions for reliefing consultants's pressure due to long work hours and high intensity workload. Hence, the goal of the company and the two founders aims at helping consultants to relax during work hours and to explore every-day happiness.
